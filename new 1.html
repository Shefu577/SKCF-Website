WHO WE ARE

SK Children Foundation is a non profit organization(NGO) that works 365 days a year to provide the best education to the underprivileged section of the society. SK Children Foundation aims to teach needy children subjects and life values that go beyond the horizon of normal schooling. We strongly believe that each and every child should get equal opportunities in terms of education and co-curricular activities.


THE STORY AS IT HAPPENED


In 2016, Raghav Sharma, a class 12th student was buying some ice cream from a stall when he encountered some kids who asked him to buy them the treats. The kids were from a nearby slum and were looking malnourished. Being his creative self, Raghav asked the kids to recite the English alphabet in exchange for ice cream. When they could not recall the alphabets, he realized something: the gap in society between the have is and the have-nots. In that very moment, he asked himself- why should not every child be given the same basic opportunities to realise his/her potential? He believed that he must be the change he wants to see in the society. Hence he took immediate action and started teaching a couple of kids around the neighbourhood in a nearby park in Janakpuri. He has not looked back ever since. The number of kids have grown from one to 150+, new dedicated members have joined the cause, volunteers have helped on many occasions and numerous events have already been held. In 2019, the NGO was formally registered as SK Children Foundation as a public charitable trust. Contact us on the mentioned email or instagram and facebook pages to connect with us. All the pictures and logs of our activities can be checked on our Instagram and Facebook pages: skchildrenfoundation.

WHAT WE DO


  • We Teach

    Our classes are conducted all 7 days a week in the morning or evening in Janakpuri park, New Delhi. We teach subjects like Maths, English, Hindi, Social sciences and more to our children who are in the 6-14 age group and come from poor backgrounds.

  • We Strengthen

    Due to the age differences, we conduct adaptive classes where each faculty member teaches a few students based on his/her level. Our hard working members make sure each and every child understands the concept well.

  • We Play

    On Sundays, we conduct an activity day where all students play sports like kho-kho, football, cricket, bowling, etc. which help them enjoy and learn. We also give coloring books to draw and colour, and conduct special meditation sessions for all the kids.

  • We Provide Food

    We serve healthy meals and refreshments to the students on a regular basis. Many a time we also provide lunch during classes. Special sweets, gifts, stationary, bags are distributed during festivals and events. All such expenses are funded by us as of now.

  • We Enjoy Festivals

    We conduct special events for every festival, be it Diwali, Holi, Dussehra, Gurupurab, Gandhi Jayanti and many more. We focus on teaching the significance and values associated with each festival and how to implement them in our life.

  • We Have Fun

    We want that students should learn while having fun. Hence we conduct educational picnics for students learning and enjoyment. Recently we have conducted excursions to Aeroplanet Dwarka, Mughal Gardens, India Gate among others.
